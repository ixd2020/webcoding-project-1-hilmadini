JESSICA WALSH
NEW YORK

About

Jessica Walsh is an American Graphic Designer, Art Director and Illustrator and the founder of Creative Agency & Walsh. She was born in New York and raised in RidgeField, Connecticut. She found her interest in this field when she was at the age of 11 and went on to study Graphic Design at the Rhode Island School of Design. Walsh found developed her personal style after working under Paula Scher and Print Magazine. Working primarily on branding, typography, website design and art installation, Walsh's style has been described as Bold, Emotional and Provocative with the occasional surrealistic flourish. She eventually formed her own studio, Creative Agency and Walsh in hopes of running her own business and turning it into a known studio in the United States.
