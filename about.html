JESSICA WALSH
NEW YORK

Jessica Walsh is an influential graphic designer. Described as bold, emotional and provocative, Walsh rose to fame for her blending handcraft, photography and painting with digital design. Not only is she known for her works but additionally for publishing the famous 40 Days of Dating: An Experiment book.

Jessica Walsh is an American Graphic Designer, Art Director and Illustrator and the founder of Creative Agency & Walsh (AndWalsh.com). She lectures about design at creative conferences and universities internationally and teaches design & typography at The School of Visual Arts in NYC.

Walsh was born in New York and raised in RidgeField, Connecticut. She found her interest in this field when she was at the age of 11 and went on to study Graphic Design at the Rhode Island School of Design. Walsh developed her personal style after working under Paula Scher and Print Magazine. She eventually formed her own studio, Creative Agency and Walsh in hopes of running her own business and turning it into a known studio in the United States. Till this day, she continues to be one of the most influential Graphic Designer in the world.
